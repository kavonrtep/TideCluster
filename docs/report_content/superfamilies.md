## TRC Superfamilies

After TRCs are established, their TAREAN consensus sequences are
compared pairwise by BLAST. TRCs whose consensus sequences share
significant similarity are grouped into **Superfamilies** — a second
level of the repeat hierarchy that captures families (TRCs) that are
themselves related.

Each non-singleton superfamily is shown with a list of member TRCs
(clickable) and a pairwise dotplot of their consensus sequences. In
the dotplot:

- Blue segments show forward-strand similarity.
- Red segments show reverse-strand similarity.
- Colour saturation reflects percent identity.

Superfamily IDs are numbered from 1 in decreasing order of member
count. Singleton TRCs (those without a significant BLAST hit to any
other TRC) do not form a superfamily and do not appear in this list.
